SHARE / CREATE / INNOVATE

We are excited to invite you to Innovation Days during the second quarter of 2016. Innovation Days will be a two-day event where people from across the organization meet to unleash creativity, develop and test new ideas – all while having fun!

Are you an “out of the box” thinker and want to make a difference? If you haven’t already, please take our survey or submit your idea. We will let you know when you can officially register.

What will you do?

As one of the participants you will:

  1. Pitch your idea - or vote on the one you believe in
  2. You are put in teams to develop the idea - in 48 hours
  3. Present what you have been working on to the jury and other participants
  4. Let the jury votes for the best ones

Why

A company in the 21st century, with ever more rapidly changing markets, cannot survive without innovation. Innovation needs to become a part of our culture and thinking - and we need a space where we can unleash our ideas, without the stress and limits of daily work life. Innovation Days have three goals:

  1. Let people from across the organization work together
  2. Make innovation a part of our internal culture
  3. Tap into the vast resource of innovation that our employees are and let them work on their latent ideas for new products, services, and improvements.

When & Where

Innovation days will take place in Q2 2016, the dates are yet to be determined.
